# Net to Dot

**From Net to Dot** is a non-commercial manifesto for digital sovereignty.

We live in a time of unprecedented connectivity.
Yet many of us use only a fraction of our personal device’s power,
while becoming increasingly dependent on networks, platforms, and algorithms
that shape our attention, choices, and lives.

Net to Dot is a simple declaration:

**We are not leaving the network.  
We are reclaiming ourselves within it.**

---

## What is Net?

**Net** represents a mode of life shaped by unconscious dependency.

- Endless feeds and algorithmic recommendations
- Passive consumption instead of active creation
- Excessive cloud reliance and data extraction
- Loss of attention, context, and agency
- Humans reduced to users, metrics, and data points

The problem is not connection itself,
but connection without sovereignty.

---

## What is Dot?

**Dot** represents a sovereign, thinking human.

A Dot is:
- An independent subject, not a user account
- A center of thought, judgment, and creation
- A person who uses technology consciously
- Someone who prefers meaning over feeds
- Someone who creates before consuming

Dot is not isolation.
Dot is not disconnection.

Dot is **center**.

---

## Our Principles

Net to Dot is founded on these principles:

- We do not collect names.
- We do not collect email addresses.
- We do not create user accounts.
- We do not run newsletters.
- We do not use advertising trackers.
- We do not monetize attention or data.

Participation is anonymous.
A Dot is not an identity.
A Dot is a declaration.

---

## Dot Spirit in Practice

This project uses only the minimum infrastructure needed to exist.

We may use tools such as:
- Cloud hosting
- Open-source repositories
- Basic routing services

But we refuse:
- Surveillance-based analytics
- Behavioral profiling
- Data-driven persuasion
- Platform lock-in disguised as “convenience”

We use the Net as infrastructure.
We do not accept the Net as our master.

---

## Join as a Dot

To join as a Dot does not mean creating an account.
It does not mean subscribing or registering.

It means choosing:

- Thought over feed
- Creation over consumption
- Sovereignty over dependency

Your Dot is not your identity.
It is your declaration.
