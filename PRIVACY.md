# Privacy Policy

Net to Dot is fundamentally opposed to surveillance-based digital life.

This project exists to help people reclaim thought, attention, and digital sovereignty.
Therefore, privacy is not an add-on.
Privacy **is the foundation**.

---

## What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- User accounts
- Passwords
- Personal profiles
- Exact addresses
- Precise GPS locations
- Advertising identifiers
- Device fingerprints
- Behavioral tracking data

There is no signup.
There is no login.
There is no newsletter.
There is no user database.

---

## What Participation Means

Participating as a Dot is **not identity registration**.

When someone chooses to participate as a Dot, we may record only:

- A general geographic region (country or city level)
- The type of participation (e.g., understanding / agreement / joining)
- The date of participation (day-level, not exact time)

This information is stored **without any personal identifiers**.

Dots on the map do not represent people.
They represent **anonymous resonance**.

---

## Analytics & Tracking

We do not use:

- Advertising trackers
- Behavioral profiling tools
- Cross-site tracking
- Third-party marketing analytics

If basic operational metrics are used in the future, they will be:
- Privacy-respecting
- Aggregate-only
- Non-identifying
- Clearly disclosed

---

## Infrastructure & Services

Net to Dot uses only minimal infrastructure required to operate:

- Domain and DNS services
- Static site hosting
- Source code repositories
- Email routing for receiving messages

These services are not used to profile visitors, build user histories, or extract behavioral data.

We use the Net as infrastructure.
We do not accept the Net as our master.

---

## Email Policy

We do not collect email addresses from visitors.

Any public contact email exists solely to receive:
- Translation offers
- Error reports
- Fact corrections
- Technical contributions

Emails received are not added to lists, shared, or repurposed.

---

## Children and Youth

Net to Dot does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone,
including children and youth.

This project does not offer accounts, targeting, or personalization.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may evolve as the project grows,
but its core principle will not change:

**We minimize data by design.**

Any meaningful changes will be clearly documented.

---

## Core Statement

Your Dot is not your identity.  
It is your declaration.

Net to Dot  
Privacy minimal.  
Human first.
