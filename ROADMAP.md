# Roadmap

This roadmap does not describe scale.
It describes intention.

Net to Dot is not driven by growth metrics,
engagement targets, or expansion plans.
It moves forward only when doing so
does not compromise its principles.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (Completed)

Establish clarity before building anything.

- Define the core manifesto
- Document privacy-first principles
- Declare operational philosophy (Dot Spirit)
- Articulate mission and direction
- Reject data collection by default
- Reject monetization by default

This phase exists to ensure
that no technical decision comes before human values.

---

## Phase 1 — Manifesto Platform

Make the ideas publicly readable and stable.

- Publish the manifesto on a minimal static site
- Support multiple manifesto versions (public, philosophical, next generation)
- Enable multiple languages, starting small
- Ensure accessibility without accounts or login
- Keep loading fast and dependencies minimal

No interactivity beyond what is necessary.
No personalization.
No tracking.

---

## Phase 2 — Anonymous Dot Participation

Allow symbolic participation without identity.

- Introduce simple “Join as a Dot” action
- Record only anonymous, regional-level participation
- Visualize participation as dots, not profiles
- Ensure participation creates no obligation
- Make non-participation equally valid

Participation is a declaration, not enrollment.

---

## Phase 3 — Practice over Promotion

Shift focus from reading to living.

- Publish Dot Practices (simple, optional actions)
- Encourage offline reflection and use
- Provide tools that reduce dependency, not increase it
- Avoid gamification, streaks, scores, or rankings

Net to Dot does not aim to occupy attention.
It aims to release it.

---

## Phase 4 — Fact Book & References

Strengthen clarity without dogmatism.

- Publish fact-based context where appropriate
- Separate evidence from interpretation
- Clearly distinguish philosophy from data
- Update cautiously and transparently

Fact Book exists to support thinking,
not to win arguments.

---

## Phase 5 — Translation & Distribution

Let the project spread only where it resonates.

- Enable community-driven translation
- Share content freely for non-commercial use
- Encourage local reinterpretation
- Avoid centralized messaging or campaigns

Resonance matters more than reach.

---

## What This Project Will Not Become

To remain aligned with Dot Spirit, Net to Dot will not become:

- A startup
- A social platform
- A content feed
- A community forum
- A subscription service
- A branding vehicle
- A data-driven product

If becoming any of these is required,
the project should stop instead.

---

## On Endings

Net to Dot accepts the possibility of ending.

There is no obligation for permanence.
There is no shame in stopping.

A project ends honorably
when it has said what needed to be said
without becoming what it opposed.

---

## Core Roadmap Statement

Net to Dot will only move forward
when the next step preserves sovereignty,
reduces dependency,
and respects human attention.

If not, it will remain still.
