Dot Spirit

Dot Spirit describes how Net to Dot is practiced, not just what it claims.

This project uses only the minimum infrastructure required to exist, while deliberately refusing surveillance, profiling, and data-driven persuasion.

Dot Spirit is defined as much by what we refuse as by what we use.

We use the Net as infrastructure. We do not accept the Net as our master.
