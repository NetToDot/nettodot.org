# Dot Spirit

Dot Spirit is the practice behind the manifesto.

Net to Dot is not only about what we say.
It is about how we operate, what we refuse, and what we deliberately limit.

Dot Spirit is the discipline of using technology
without surrendering human agency to it.

---

## Our Position

Net to Dot does not reject the Internet.
We do not reject networks, cloud infrastructure, or modern tools.

We reject **unconscious dependency**.

We believe the problem is not connection itself,
but connection without choice, awareness, or sovereignty.

Dot Spirit is not anti-technology.
Dot Spirit is **pro-human**.

---

## Minimal Infrastructure Principle

This project uses only the minimum infrastructure required to exist.

We intentionally avoid complexity that leads to:
- Data accumulation
- Behavioral profiling
- Platform lock-in
- Growth-driven surveillance

Infrastructure is a tool.
It must never become a master.

---

## Tools We Use (Intentionally)

As of now, Net to Dot uses:

- Domain & DNS services
- Static site hosting
- Open-source version control
- Email routing for receiving messages

These tools are chosen for:
- Simplicity
- Transparency
- Reliability
- Minimal data retention

We do not use tools whose business model depends on
tracking, profiling, or manipulating human behavior.

---

## Things We Do Not Do

Dot Spirit is defined as much by refusal as by action.

We do not:

- Require user accounts
- Collect email addresses
- Run newsletters
- Track individuals
- Use advertising analytics
- Optimize for engagement metrics
- Design for addiction or retention
- Treat people as users, leads, or funnels

Growth is not our goal.
Awareness is.

---

## Participation Without Ownership

Participation in Net to Dot creates no account,
no profile, and no obligation.

There is nothing to subscribe to.
There is nothing to unlock.
There is nothing to monetize.

A Dot is not owned by this project.
A Dot belongs to the person who chooses it.

---

## On Scale and Growth

We do not pursue scale for its own sake.

If Net to Dot spreads,
it should spread by resonance, not promotion.

If it grows,
it should grow by understanding, not virality.

If it disappears,
it disappears honestly.

---

## Accountability

Dot Spirit requires consistency.

If we ever:
- Add tracking
- Introduce data collection
- Build systems that undermine sovereignty

Then this document must be updated,
and the change must be made explicit.

Dot Spirit is not static.
But its direction is fixed.

---

## Core Statement

We use the Net as infrastructure.  
We do not accept the Net as our master.

Technology serves humans.  
Humans do not serve technology.

Your Dot is not your identity.  
It is your declaration.
